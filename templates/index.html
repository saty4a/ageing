Introducing Ageing Signs Detection

Ageing Signs Detection is a face recognition solution that is able to detect the dark spots, puffy eyes, and wrinkles on the face of both genders in real time and gives a veracious report. The web page which will take an image as an input.

Image processing techniques is used to process the image as per requirements. The model required to work upon is EfficientNet which will detect signs of ageing like puffy eyes, wrinkles, dark spots on the image. Overlay of bounding boxes around detected ageing signs.
